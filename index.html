About
Plover Hill Capital offers tailored solutions to help advisors protect their legacy, fuel growth, and manage transitions smoothly. We offer minority capital, transition financing, and expert guidance, empowering you to achieve your goals.
We seek to disrupt industry status quos, specifically that internal succession from G1 to G2 is neither economically or practically feasible​. Nor do we believe independent advisory firms cannot succeed in this period of mass aggregation.
In fact, we believe that the gradual maturation of the independent financial advisory space over the past 20 years has provided a unique opportunity to address a market for firms seeking to protect their independence​. Such trends include:
  • The mix of the influx of G2 talent and poor recruitment and retention of G2 by G1​ and mega-RIAs
  • Commoditization of technology solutions and myriad industry services once attainable only through expensive outsourcing engagements
  • Transparency and education around valuation, deal structure and optionality in the industry
    • PHC will provide tailored capital solutions and advice to independent advisors seeking guidance with strategy, growth, succession, and transitions​.

    Founder Discount & Financing
  • Internal succession facilitated by G1 either taking a meaningful discount to market value or extended financing period
  • Financing frequently provided by seller themselves through the form of a seller note
  • G1 retains majority of risk while receiving meaningful discount to fair value
    • SBA Loan
  • G2 secures SBA (or similar) loan to acquire practice from G1
  • Personal guarantee required, along with other covenants
  • At current fair market valuations, G2 unable to service the note purely using cash flow from operations
  • G2 required to use outside capital to service debt, making this impractical in most cases

    PHC Succession Solution
  • PCH designed mezzanine-like succession solution that creates flexible structure that aligns incentives and provides G1 with fair market value for shares
  • 5 year interest-only period, followed by fixed 10 year term loan allows G2 the time and flexibility to grow the business

  • Fair market value received by G1 with achievable capital terms for G2 allowing for successful internal transition
